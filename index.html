Welcome to Gentle Warrior

Gentle Warrior is inspired by the journey of a small child who faced early medical challenges with a courage far bigger than his body.

This universe is built around kindness, resilience, and mythic symbols of the heart. Over time, we’ll grow stories, games, and on-chain rewards that stay true to that core.

What we’re building:

  • A positive, heart-centered brand and hero.
  • Stories and art that families can enjoy together.
  • Simple games with fair, transparent rewards.
  • A community that treats kindness as a power-up.
