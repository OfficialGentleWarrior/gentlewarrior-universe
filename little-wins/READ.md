# 🌱 Little Wins

> Celebrate every little win, no matter how small.

Little Wins is a community platform where people can document and celebrate milestones throughout their journey. It is designed to be inclusive of all ages and welcomes individuals, families, caregivers, and supporters across different disability and developmental communities.
